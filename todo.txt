add question weights
    for example lets take a quiz about fruits with 5 questions
    (question #) (question type) (possible points) (score %) (points earned)
    (1) (select all that apply) (7) (0.8) (5.6)
    (2) (multiple choice) (2) (0) (0)
    (3) (number) (10) (1) (10)

    (5) (text) (3) (1) (3)

    calculate earned points for each question by multiple total by score then rounding to 2 decimal places
        1: 7 * 0.8 = 5.6
        2: 2 * 0 = 0
        3: 10 * 1 = 10
        4: 3 * 0.66 = 2
        5: 3 * 1 = 3
    we add the earned points 5.6 + 0 + 10 + 2 + 3 = 15.6 + 5 = 20.6
    we get the overall grade by dividing earned points by total points and rounding to 2 decimal places 20.6 / 25 = 0.824 = 0.82
